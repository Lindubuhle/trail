Lindo Ngodwane

For Parliament Member

Born in 1997, Lindo Ngodwane, a seasoned diplomat and strategist, has dedicated her career to international cooperation.

With a doctorate in international relations, she has served as an ambassador to three provinces, fostering alliances and negotiating agreements. As a senior advisor at the University of Cape Town, Lindo works tirelessly to address poverty, education, and crime. Her commitment to these areas continues to shape a better tomorrow.

"Shaping a better tomorrow through dedication and service."
