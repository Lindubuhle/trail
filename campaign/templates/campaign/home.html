About Lindo Ngodwane

Born in 1997, Lindo Ngodwane is a seasoned diplomat and strategist dedicated to international cooperation and community development.

Lindo holds a doctorate in international relations and has served as an ambassador to three provinces, fostering alliances and negotiating agreements that benefit our community.

As a senior advisor at the University of Cape Town, Lindo works tirelessly to address poverty, education, and crime, shaping a better tomorrow for all.
